A Better Look at Our Boards!

Board Type Description
Foam Board Our Foam Board's are surfboards made from high density foam with a slick plastic base, they are stable, forgiving to surf and are perfect for any beginning surfers or surfers who are looking for a lower price board for a bit of fun.
Mini Mal Our Mini Mal surfboards or funboards are the type of boards we recommend for any surfer who is looking to progress there technique whilst still allowing more manoevurability than a standard longboard. The shape of this board normally is a reference to boards over 7'6 with a traditional longboard shape, which allow for stable take offs and nose riding potential.
LongBoard Our Longboard surfboards are the standard 8 feet long, which feature big, rounded noses, wide decks, and that have a little extra thickness for increased volume when surfing.
Funboard Our Funboard surfboards are between 6-8 feet long which have a rounder and wider outline than shortboards.
Fish Our fishtail surfboards come in all shapes in sizes. Our boards range from 5ft to longboard size. Typically we recommend using these boards on smaller waves comapred to larger 8ft waves.
ShortBoard Our shortboard surfboards are recommedned for our athletes who want maximum performance in good to epic surf conditions. We recommedned them for expirenced surfacces, as without the proper skill of surfing will slow down the progression of intermediate surfers across the world.
Gun Our Gun surfboards feature a continous free flowing curve running on the outline without any straight sections or hips. These boards allow for smoother transitions between turns, and they are also used to rude larger than life waves. These surfboards surf extremely well in really big waves.
Standup PaddleBoard Our standup PaddleBoards our perfect for anyone who is looking for an outdoor water sport where they can stand up the whole time and use a paddle as they glide through the water.
