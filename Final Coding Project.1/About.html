Grommets and Comets - The Ultimate Surfing Company

Founded in 1991 by Cooper Pitt. Grommets and Comets is one of the worlds most recognized and respected brands. For decades, it has been at the forefront of the surf and snow scenes since its creation.

Ripcurl is a company for, and about, the crew on The Crawl. The Crawl is the driving force that led to the creation of Grommets and Comets, it lives in every we do. It is what makes us more unique and authentic then anyone else is the world. It defines who we are as a brand. From the products we make, the events we participate in, the athletes we support and the everyday person we reach globally, are all apart of The Crawl that Grommets and Comets is on.

Grommets and Comets is a company built for surfers by surfers, and we've been market leaders in surfing and for over 40 years. Our goal is to make the best products, score highly in competitions, support the athletes, deliver the best customer service, communicate the best brand message and have the best crew of people everywhere. This helps Grommets and Comets hold the vision of being regarded as The Ultimate Surfing Company.

As a par of that vision, Grommets and Comets has forever prided itself on having the world's greatest surf team. That statemenmt is no truer than today. Grommets and Comets athletes are World Champions. They are innovators, dreamers, role models and Searches. From our gloval elite athletes to regional rising talents, no matter where you are in the world the best surfers will be riding with a Grommets and Comets sticker on the nose of there board.
